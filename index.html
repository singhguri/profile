About me

Hi there! I'm Gurpreet Singh, a passionate Software Engineer with a Master's degree in Applied Computing specialized from the University of Windsor.

I'm an expert in Full-Stack Development, having over 2 years of work experience at Capgemini India where I worked on various frameworks such as Spring-Boot, Angular/NodeJS, React and Kafka, to name a few. While working at Cap, I had the opportunity to collaborate with talented developers on creating innovative solutions. My role allowed me to develop strong leadership abilities while working in a team environment.

I have been passionate about coding since I was in school, starting with Java and C++ and going all the way towards learning C, Python and Javascript at undergraduate level which enabled me to expand my knowledge across various frameworks such as Spring-Boot, Django, MERN Stack, HTML/CSS with PHP, etc. I became familar with CI/CD while working at Cap, by using tools such as Docker, Kubernetees, Microsoft Azure, AWS(Amazon Web Services) and Git.

I use my interest as a motivation to constantly upgrade my knowledge and contribute to the fast-evolving sphere of Information Technolgy. With the advancements in AI in recent times I am constantly keeping myself updating by learning about Generative AI, LLMs, and various Machine Learning concepts while also trying to make as many open source contributions as possible to keep myself ahead in a competitive job market and diversify my skill set.

I'm always eager to collaborate with passionate professionals and entrepreneurs. Please feel free to connect with me here to exchange ideas!
